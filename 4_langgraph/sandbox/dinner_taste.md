# Dinner Report: Chinese Restaurants in Bellevue, WA

## 1. Din Tai Fung
- **Address:** 700 110th Ave NE, Bellevue, WA 98004
- **Phone:** (425) 214-7988
- **Menu Highlights:**
  - Braised Beef Noodle Soup - $20.00
  - Pork Chop Fried Rice - $20.00
  - Kurobuta Pork Xiao Long Bao (10) - $17.50
  - String Beans with Garlic - $16.00
- **Reviews:**
  Din Tai Fung is renowned for its xiaolongbao and has received positive reviews for its quick service and quality dishes. While some reviewers noted that the standard steamed dumplings are not the best, highlights include the crab soup dumplings and beef noodle soup. Overall, it's a must-visit if you love dumplings!

---

## 2. Facing East
- **Address:** 14600 NE 40th St, Bellevue, WA 98007
- **Phone:** (425) 643-2229
- **Menu Highlights:**
  - Taiwanese Pork Burger
  - Crispy Soy-Flavored Chicken
  - Braised Pork Chop on Rice
- **Reviews:**
  Facing East offers an authentic taste of Taiwanese cuisine. Popular dishes like the pork burgers and sesame flatbread omelette have received praise from diners. Customers also appreciate the quick service and friendly staff, making it a popular choice for a casual dining experience.

---

## 3. Dough Zone Dumpling House
- **Address:** 14625 NE 24th St, Ste 4B, Bellevue, WA 98007
- **Phone:** (425) 641-5555
- **Menu Highlights:**
  - Soup Dumplings
  - Pork & Shrimp Dumplings
  - Dan Dan Noodles
- **Reviews:**
  Known for its delightful dumplings and prompt service, Dough Zone is a great spot for families. Signature dishes such as the chicken soup dumplings and green onion pancakes are particularly recommended. Diners note that service is always attentive with a quick turnaround for food preparation.

---

Plan to visit any of these restaurants for a delicious Chinese dining experience in Bellevue!

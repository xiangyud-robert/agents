# Banoffee Pie Recipe

An easy family favorite with buttery pastry and sweet dulce de leche. Served best with a generous dollop of cream and optional dark chocolate garnish.

## Ingredients
- 4 bananas, sliced
- 394g caramel or dulce de leche
- 300ml double cream
- Dark chocolate (optional)

### For the pastry
- 100g butter, chilled (plus extra for greasing)
- 200g plain flour
- 1 medium egg, separated
- 1 tbsp golden caster sugar

## Method
1. Make the pastry case: Put butter and flour in a food processor and pulse until it resembles fresh breadcrumbs. Add egg yolk and sugar and pulse again until combined. Add very cold water a tbsp at a time, pulsing after each addition until dough comes together.
2. Knead dough gently, smooth into a ball, wrap in cling film, and chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.
3. Heat oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 4. Grease a 23cm loose-bottomed fluted tart tin. Roll out pastry on a floured surface and line the tin, leaving about half a cm over the edge, trim with scissors.
4. Line base with baking parchment and weigh down with baking beans or uncooked rice. Bake for 15 minutes, then remove paper and beans. Brush pastry with beaten egg white and bake again for 15-20 minutes until crisp and golden. Cool completely.
5. Spread half of the caramel over the pastry case, layer sliced bananas on top, cover with remaining caramel, and chill in the fridge.
6. Whip the cream until thick and billowy, spoon over the tart. Garnish with finely grated dark chocolate if desired.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Banoffee Pie!

### Chosen Company for Investment

**Apilayer**

- **Reason for Selection:**  
  Apilayer was chosen primarily due to its strong market position in the rapidly expanding API management sector. The company has demonstrated substantial growth potential, driven by increasing demand for digital business transformations. Its comprehensive and easy-to-integrate APIs have established it as a leader in its field. This market positioning and growth trajectory make Apilayer an attractive investment opportunity, offering promising returns for investors looking to capitalize on tech innovation.

### Companies Not Selected

**Workai**

- **Reason for Non-Selection:**  
  While Workai presents a compelling opportunity within the AI technology sector, focusing on automating tasks for organizational efficiency, it carries a higher risk profile due to its emerging status. Although it holds significant potential for high returns, the company is still establishing itself compared to more structured competitors in the automation industry.

**Konnecto**

- **Reason for Non-Selection:**  
  Konnecto, operating in the marketing technology sector, shows excellent innovation, particularly with its recent acquisition by Partnerize. However, despite its exciting future outlook post-acquisition, the competitive landscape within affiliate marketing intelligence could limit its immediate growth and market capture relative to established giants, making it less advantageous for short-term investment.
